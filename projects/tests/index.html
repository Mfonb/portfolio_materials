Characterisation Projects.

December 2021


Brief

The project involved the study of polymer materials suitable for drug delivery. Certain medical devices display functionalities for suitability for use/application. This project required materials identification for characterisation to determine appropriateness for a drug delivery application. Other projects on characterisation focussed on studies of metal materials which is not described here in detail. The project involved an intense application/study of SEM techniques such as the EDS and EBSD techniques.
